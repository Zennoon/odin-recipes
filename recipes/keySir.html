Key Sir

Description

If you love Ethiopian food, this key sir alicha is a must-try. This is one of my favorite dishes. Serve hot over injera to soak up the sauce.

This dish will be ready to eat in an hour and is sufficient for 4 people.

Fun fact: The phrase Key Sir is the Amharic word for beet root and it translates to red root.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, diced
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh garlic
  • 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger (Optional)
  • 2 large beets, diced
  • 1 cup water
  • 4 large potatoes, diced
